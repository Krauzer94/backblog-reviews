## Old-School FPS Roots
The third Serious Sam entry is a FPS that aims to deliver old-school, high-octane action. Developed by Croteam and published by Devolver Digital, it features massive waves of enemies and a large arsenal of weapons, so no news on this regard, it works similarly as the previous entries in the frachise. I confess that this one was my first game of this series, I've never played the previous ones, eve though I'm a big fan of oldschool shooters such as Doom, Quake and alike.

## Overwhelming Combat Loop
On paper, the relentless combat and fast-paced gameplay sound appealing, but in practice, it quickly becomes overwhelming and repetitive. The level design emphasizes constant fighting over any meaningful narrative or variety, which made it hard for me to stay engaged.
